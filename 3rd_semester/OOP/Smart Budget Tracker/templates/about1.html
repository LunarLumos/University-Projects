About Smart Budget Tracker

This is my university project under Daffodil University, Teacher: Ajaam Khan

Project Overview

Smart Budget Tracker is a comprehensive personal finance management application designed to help users track their income, expenses, and budgets in one convenient place. With beautiful visual reports and intelligent budget alerts, users can gain valuable insights into their spending habits and make informed financial decisions.

Features

Income & Expense Tracking

Easily record and categorize all your financial transactions.

Budget Management

Set daily, monthly, or yearly budgets for different spending categories.

Visual Reports

Interactive charts and graphs to visualize your financial data.

Budget Alerts

Get notified when your spending in any category exceeds 40% of your income.

Responsive Design

Access your financial data from any device with a beautiful, modern interface.

Technology Stack

Backend

Python, Flask, SQLite

Frontend

HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Chart.js

Design

Custom CSS with luxurious gradients, shadows, and animations

Developer

This project was developed as part of the curriculum at Daffodil University under the guidance of Teacher Ajaam Khan. The application demonstrates proficiency in full-stack web development, database design, user interface design, and software engineering principles.
